Teapot

You may use the Skybox, or Teapot controls to control the view of the scene. Choose between 3 options on how to shade the teapot with Phong, Reflective, or Refraction shading.

The resolution of the canvas may also be adjusted by modifying the width, and height values.

Please wait a few seconds for the images to load. I did not want to compress the images further as I wanted the highest quality possible being displayed.

Tip: Hold down the rotation buttons to rotate smoothly across the scene.
